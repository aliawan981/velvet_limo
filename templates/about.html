Company Overview

Velvet Essence Limousine Services LLC is a premier luxury transportation company based in New Rochelle, New York, proudly serving Westchester County, New York City, the Tri- State Area, and Rochelle, New York, proudly serving Westchester County, New York City, the Tri- State Area, and all major airports. Conveniently located just minutes from Westchester County Airport (HPN), we provide prompt access to JFK, LaGuardia (LGA), Newark Liberty (EWR), and all other surrounding airports.

We specialize in airport transfers, corporate travel, weddings, special events, and private chauffeur services. With luxury vehicles, professional chauffeurs, and a commitment to exceptional service, we deliver safe, reliable, and first-class transportation for every journey.

Our Vision

To be the premier luxury limousine and chauffeur service in Westchester County and the Tri- State Area, recognized for excellence, reliability, and an exceptional customer experience on every journey.

Our Mission

At Velvet Essence Limousine Services LLC, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and luxury transportation with professionalism, punctuality, and personalized service. We are committed to exceeding our clients' expectations by delivering comfortable, stress-free travel for airport transfers, corporate transportation, special events, and every occasion in between.
